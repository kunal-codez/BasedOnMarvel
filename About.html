About Marvel

Marvel Comics is an American comic book publisher, known for its iconic characters such as Spider-Man, Iron Man, and the X-Men. Founded in 1939, Marvel has become one of the most influential publishers in the industry.

Marvel's stories often explore themes of heroism, identity, and the struggle between good and evil. The Marvel Universe is a complex and interconnected world where characters frequently cross paths, leading to epic storylines and collaborations.

Over the years, Marvel has expanded beyond comics into movies, television shows, and merchandise, becoming a major cultural phenomenon worldwide.
