Marvel Studios

Marvel Studios is the film and television production company responsible for creating the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). Founded in 1993 as Marvel Films, it was rebranded as Marvel Studios in 1996. The studio is a subsidiary of Walt Disney Studios, which acquired Marvel Entertainment in 2009.
