Characters

Iron Man

Tony Stark, a genius inventor and billionaire industrialist, becomes a superhero after being kidnapped and forced to build a weapon, but instead creates a suit of armor to escape and protect others.

Captain America

Steve Rogers, a frail young man, is transformed into the super-soldier Captain America during World War II, fighting for justice and freedom with his enhanced strength, agility, and indomitable spirit.

Thor

Thor, the Norse God of Thunder, wields the enchanted hammer Mjolnir and possesses immense strength, using his powers to protect both Asgard and Earth from various threats.

Black Widow

Natasha Romanoff, a highly skilled spy and assassin, uses her expertise in combat, espionage, and tactics to fight alongside the Avengers and protect the world from dangerous adversaries.

Doctor Strange

Stephen Strange, a brilliant surgeon turned Sorcerer Supreme, masters the mystic arts to protect the world from supernatural threats and maintain the balance between dimensions.

Hulk

Bruce Banner, a brilliant scientist, transforms into the Hulk, a green-skinned behemoth with immense strength and durability, whenever he experiences extreme anger or stress.

Spiderman

Peter Parker, a brilliant teenager, gains spider-like abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider, using his powers to fight crime and protect his city.
